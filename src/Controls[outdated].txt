+----------+
|  Basic   |
+----------+

Mouse:
Left Drag		Move view
Right Drag		Change time

Keyboard:
Ctrl+A			Add constellation
Ctrl+D			Delete constellation
A			Add line to current constellation
D			Delete line from current constellation
S			Start rotating starfield



+----------+
|   Fun    |
+----------+

Mouse:
Ctrl + Right Drag	Change latitude
Shift + Right Drag	Move sun independently from stars

Keyboard:
F			Fog on/off
E			External terrain view on/off
NumPad +		Move terrain down
NumPad -		Move terrain up
