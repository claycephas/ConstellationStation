--------
CONTROLS
--------

When Viewing:
  Left click to rotate the view
  Right click to rotate the stars according to time
  Z / X or mouse wheel to zoom
  
When Adding Lines:
  Left click on stars to make a line
  Right click to start a new line
  Right click again to stop adding lines

When Adding Polygon:
  Left click on stars to make lines of polygon
  Right click to close polygon
  Right click again to stop adding polygons

When Deleting Lines:
  Left click on line to delete
